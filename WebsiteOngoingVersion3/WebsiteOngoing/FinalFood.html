Your Local Favorite Food

  • Lechon Kawali

    Lechon Kawali is a Filipino classic that features golden, crispy pork belly fried to perfection. Served with a side of rich liver sauce, vinegar, and spicy dipping sauce, it offers a savory and indulgent experience for your taste buds.

    Perfectly paired with a steaming bowl of rice or a fresh, crunchy salad, this dish is a must-try for those who love hearty and flavorful meals. Whether you're sharing with friends or enjoying it solo, Lechon Kawali never disappoints.

  • Halo Halo

    Halo Halo is a beloved Filipino dessert known for its vibrant mix of ingredients. Shaved ice, sweetened beans, fruits, and leche flan come together to create a colorful and refreshing treat that’s perfect for warm weather.

    The final touch is a scoop of creamy ube ice cream on top, which adds a unique sweetness to the medley of flavors. Whether you're enjoying it on a hot summer day or as a sweet ending to your meal, Halo Halo is guaranteed to satisfy your cravings.

  • Puso Rice

    Puso Rice is a unique and traditional Filipino rice dish, wrapped neatly in coconut leaves. This eco-friendly packaging not only helps preserve the flavor but also gives the rice a subtle, slightly sweet aroma that enhances its taste.

    Often served with grilled meats or seafood, Puso Rice is the perfect companion for those looking to enjoy a hearty, flavorful meal. Whether at a fiesta or a simple gathering, it’s always a crowd-pleaser and a symbol of Filipino culture.

  • Seafood

    Our Seafood dish features a delicious blend of fresh shrimp, squid, and fish, all cooked in a savory, flavorful sauce. The seafood is tender, juicy, and infused with a variety of spices that elevate the natural flavors of the ingredients.

    Perfectly complemented with a side of rice or enjoyed on its own, this dish is ideal for seafood lovers. It's a fulfilling meal that offers both variety and freshness in every bite, making it a standout choice for any occasion.

About

Moalboal, located on the southwestern coast of Cebu, is a paradise known for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine life. Famous for the stunning Sardine Run, it offers world-class diving and snorkeling experiences.
